V-GNYANA - THIRD EDITION OF VIRTUAL QUIZ

With the immense support and encouragement from LTI, the virtual quizzing event is conducted in 3 rounds covering a breadth of topics - About India, Current Affairs, Health & Hygiene, and Sports across 146 schools from 8 States (Andhra Pradesh, Jharkhand, Maharashtra, Karnataka, Tamil Nadu, Uttar Pradesh, Uttarakhand, and Telangana ) reaching 3000+ students in teams of 3 students.
